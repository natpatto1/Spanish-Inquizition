The Spanish Inquizition is a free browser based application for English speakers to learn and memorize Spanish vocabulary and phrases. I have tried to make learning fun, not torturous! Along the way you will pick up some useful language points you can apply to other sources of learning.

I have built a prototype of 4 topics for beginners. The aim of the game is to earn points and unlock new topics by playing games that test words or phrases you haven't mastered.

By the end of the course you will know more about how to talk about yourself, other people, food and ordering and counting. Venga! Let's go!
